My role

I worked on this feature in 2022 as a Product Designer for Talkdesk. Talkdesk is a global cloud contact center leader, with a big focus on automation. My team was responsible for the Healthcare product that helps organizations create better and personalized experiences for patients and caregivers.

I was responsible for the research, interaction design and visual design for the feature "Notifications" - messaging at scale.

The problem

The agent needs to send a message to a group of patients to, for example, let them know the physician is running late. Or to let them know that the clinic has had a flood and that it will be closed for a week. Our product doesn't support that type of action. Instead of sending a message to a group, the agent needs to call everyone. Besides that, the agent also wastes a lot of time calling patients to confirm appointments, to schedule referrals, and others.

The user

The target user is a call center administrator, that has a big knowledge of contact center software and tools and a deep understanding on business processes. Their main task is to ensure high-quality customer service.

The process

In order to solve the problem in an efficient and agile way, my team had developed a MVP. This way the feature would be delivered in time and when we got feedback from the users and checked the metrics, we could improve it over time.

The goal

We wanted to give the clients the agility to send a message to a group of people in an automated way. When certain events happened this would trigger and send the message to the person or group of people related with that event. This kind of automation will increase the agent productivity, letting them available for more important tasks.


Success metrics

  • Agent productivity revenue increase

  • Cost per interaction decreases

  • Number of calls to the call center decreases

How might we & Ideation

Possible solutions

  • A notification should be sent automatically whenever a state changes. Eg. A referral is pending and changes to active.

  • Help Admin fill the form with templates, hints or analytical data.

  • Send notifications through different channels according to patients preference.

MVP definition

The admin can create an Automated notification (AN), edit or delete it. This AN will be a rule responsible for sending the message to a group of patients, that will be linked to an event, like the dentist appointment date.


The admin can define the days and the time that the patient will receive the notification. For example, from mondays to saturdays, from 9AM to 6PM. They can also define if is one time notification, or if the notification will be sent everyday, every week or every month.
