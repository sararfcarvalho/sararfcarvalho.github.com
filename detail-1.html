Talkdesk is a global cloud contact center leader, with a big focus on automation.

As a Product Designer in Talkdesk I am responsible for participating in immersive co-creation sessions where we sit down with product experts and stakeholders to dive into the product vision from a business standpoint.

The products I work in are specialised in industries verticals such as Healthcare, Retail, Banking and Insurance. Besides the stakeholders sessions we have a analytics tool to understand the adoption and engagement of the users, we do interviews with clients and their agents, and some other research methods. With info collected from various sources we start to understand the problems and we try to get to the solutions and improve the user journey. I always fight to offer the best user experience possible to the users.

We work on the new features or improvements with wireframes, mockups and prototypes, always aligning with engineers and refining the designs. The work doesn’t end after the engineering team starts to code, at this point is necessary to evaluate the UI and the UX of the feature implemented, is necessary to look at the metrics defined and understand if our users are engaging with the product. If the results aren’t what we want, is time to refine the problem and work in a better solution.

For privacy reasons I can't share here what I have been doing, but if you are interested in know more let's talk!
