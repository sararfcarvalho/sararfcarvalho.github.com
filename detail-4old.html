This case study was developed in about 8 - 9 hours as an exercise for a job application.
Home automation refers to the automatic and electronic control of household features, activity, and appliances. Imagine a home where everything, from lighting, climate, air conditioning, appliances, even the alarm system, can be controlled with one tap.

Personas


Filipe - 35 yo, Video Editor, Lisbon

Filipe lives with his girlfriend, Joana, in their apartment in Lisbon. He is allways on the lookout for new technologies and products that can speed up his routine.

Frustrations: When on vacations they are always afraid that their apartment will be robbed.

Goals: He wants to reduce his and his girlfriend ecological footprint.

Frustrations VS Solutions


Some frustrations from my persona and from competitive analysis lead me to different solutions.


  • - Learnig curve; Quick controls with several modes and configurations, as the “WakeUp” mode: the blinds rise and the coffee machine turns on.

  • - Sometimes when the devices are found automatically, it results in incorrect information; The user can choose if devices are added automatically or manually.

  • - Electricity consumptions; Updated and easily understandable energy comsumption reports.

Wireframes


After studying the user flow and some sketchs I've started with the wireframes.


Home contains a day summary, with the outside and inside temperature and energy consumptions. The user can also view more detailed information. Notifications alert the user of the ocurring activities, always with the possibility to turn off the device in specific. At the bottom of this page, it shows a few Pack controls, these packs have multiple actions already configured, to save the user time.


Controls lets the user control every smart electronic device inside his home. The devices can be sorted by rooms or listed fully, in the separated ROOMS and DEVICES tabs. This allows for a faster experience for users with many devices to easily find one, and users that have few can find them in devices section.


Schedule allows the user to create events, by selecting the device, the associated room(s), and choosing the preferred schedule. The form will have some variants depending on the device. The user has the possibility to choose more than one room because he might want to turn on the AC in the bedroom and dining room.


More lets the user edit his account details, he can see the notifications history and the consumptions statistics, manage permissions of others house members or remove them, and ask for support.
